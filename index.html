My story of being forced to place an employee on a sham PIP

As a newly appointed engineering manager at Amazon, I encountered a challenging situation when I faced pressure from the leadership to place one of my direct reports on a sham performance improvement plan. This particular incident made me realize the importance of offering support during critical moments in someone's career like going through a performance improvement plan.

As an experienced engineering manager, I look forward to sharing valuable insights, and provide coaching, guidance and support to to help those facing career challenges, such as performance improvement plans, to reach a more favorable outcome.
